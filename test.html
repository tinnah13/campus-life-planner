Regex Catalog (5 rules + 1 advanced):

  • Title: /^\S(?:.*\S)?$/ - No leading/trailing spaces
  • Duration: /^(0|[1-9]\d*)(\.\d{1,2})?$/ - Positive number, optional 2 decimals
  • Date: /^\d{4}-(0[1-9]|1[0-2])-(0[1-9]|[12]\d|3[01])$/ - YYYY-MM-DD format
